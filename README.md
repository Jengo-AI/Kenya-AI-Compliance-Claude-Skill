# Kenya Legal Compliance Skill for Claude

A Claude skill that gives AI assistants comprehensive knowledge of Kenyan laws and regulations relevant to software developers, AI builders, fintech founders, and digital product creators.

## What This Skill Covers

| Domain | Laws Covered |
|---|---|
| **Data Protection** | Data Protection Act 2019, ODPC Regulations 2021 |
| **AI Regulation** | AI Bill 2026 (Senate), National AI Strategy 2025–2030 |
| **Cybersecurity** | Computer Misuse & Cybercrimes Act 2018 |
| **Fintech** | CBK Digital Credit Providers Regulations 2022, NPSA, POCAMLA 2009 |
| **Consumer Protection** | Consumer Protection Act 2012 |
| **Healthtech** | ODPC Health Data Guidance Note, Health Act |
| **General SaaS** | Business registration, privacy policies, terms of service |
